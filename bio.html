From Rosario To the World Stage

Born in June 24, 1987 in Rosario,Argentina. Played for Newells Old Boys the Machine of the 80s.

How he Rose to Fame?

He first of all joined a local club Grandoli at 5 then later on joined Newells Old Boys where he was diagnosed with a Growth Hormone Deficiency. He later joined Barcelona's Prestigous youth Academy La Masia where he was given injections to help with the Growth Hormone Deficiency.He was promoted to the barca B team before he finally debuted for the A-team in 2004 where he found instant chemistry with Ronaldhino who was his role model to him and even assisted him in his first goal for Barcelona.

His Journey from Youngster to Football Legend

Leo Messi was called up for the First time for international duty in 2005. He lead the team to back to back finals but never won a single trophy for the national team. Meanwhile he graduallyy developed into a world class player for Barcelona and he won the golden boy trophy in 2005 when he was 18 years of age. He then went on to winning his first Ballon d'or and created a record for the most consecutive ballon d'ors(4).He completed football with Barcelona but was critised for winning nothing with the national team to a point where he almost retired from national football. Finally in 2021 he broke the spell by winning the Copa America and officially completed football.
