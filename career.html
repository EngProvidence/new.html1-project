MESSI CAREER PATH

Lionel Messi has an interesting career path. He played for Barcelona, PSG and now at Inter Miami.

Life at Barcelona

Leo Messi's Career started here at Barca. He started from the La Masia youth academy and was later on promoted to the first team. He then went on to win so many trophies with the catalan giants. Barca was just like home to him. He was good friends with Neymar and Suarez and they formed what was known as MSN one of the deadliest attacking trios in europe. They made barcelona the only club in europe to win the continental treble twice. Still in Barca he became the highest goal scorer in laliga and Barceolona's all time top scorer.

Messi acheived alot with Barcelona. His best performance in his prime came from his time in Barca.
